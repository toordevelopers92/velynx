# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free environment.

## Expected behavior

- Be considerate and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Respect differing opinions and backgrounds.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Intimidation, threats, or deliberate disruption.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers at security@velynx.dev.
